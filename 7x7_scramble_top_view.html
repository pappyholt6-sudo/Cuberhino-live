7x7 Top Face Viewer

Paste a standard 7x7 scramble and this will show what the top face looks like after the scramble.

Top / U Face
Supported moves: U, D, R, L, F, B and common 7x7 wide moves like Rw, Uw, Fw, 3Rw, 3Uw, and lowercase r, u, f, each with optional 2 or ' suffix. Rotations, slice-only moves, and parentheses are not included.
