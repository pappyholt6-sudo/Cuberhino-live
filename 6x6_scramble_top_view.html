6x6 Top Face Viewer

Paste a standard 6x6 scramble and this will show what the top face looks like after the scramble.

Top / U Face
Assumes standard cube orientation: White = U/top, Yellow = D/bottom, Green = F/front, Blue = B/back, Red = R/right, Orange = L/left. The top view is shown with Blue/back at the top and Green/front at the bottom.
