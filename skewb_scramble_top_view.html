Skewb Top Face Viewer

Paste a Skewb scramble and this will show the top face after the scramble. This version keeps the white / green / red corner fixed in front while scrambling. That means the fixed corner stays at the front-right corner of the top face, so one white corner is always visible there.

Top Face (fixed WGR corner in front)
Supported moves: R, L, U, B and primes like R'.
Color scheme: U = white, D = yellow, F = green, B = blue, R = red, L = orange.
In the solved state the top face is all white. In this version, from solved state, an R move brings green to the top-right corner while the white/green/red corner stays fixed in front.
The L move is also adjusted so it affects the front-left side, not the back-left corner, with L bringing blue to the top and L' bringing red to the top.
