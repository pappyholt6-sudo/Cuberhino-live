Pyraminx Front Face Viewer

Paste a Pyraminx scramble and this will show the front face after the scramble. This version keeps the fixed orientation: yellow on bottom and green on front, with a little less black between pieces.

Front Face (green front, yellow bottom)
Fixed starting orientation: Front = green, Left of green = red, Right of green = blue, Bottom = yellow. Supported moves: U L R B, prime versions like U', and tip moves u l r b, u', etc.
